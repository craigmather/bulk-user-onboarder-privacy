Privacy Policy

Actionstep Bulk User Onboarder
Effective Date: May 12, 2026

Overview

Actionstep Bulk User Onboarder is a browser extension designed to assist administrators with bulk onboarding users within the Actionstep platform.

Information Processed

The extension may process the following information locally within the user's browser:

  • Contact IDs
  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • User role selections
  • Rate selections
  • Income account selections
  • CSV files uploaded by the user
  • Actionstep form and dropdown data

This information is processed solely for the purpose of onboarding users into the user's Actionstep environment.

Data Storage

The extension does not permanently store personal or sensitive information outside of the user's browser.

Uploaded CSV files and retrieved Actionstep data are processed temporarily in memory during use.

Generated reports are downloaded directly to the user's device and are not transmitted externally.

Data Transmission

The extension communicates directly with the user's authenticated Actionstep environment using the user's existing browser session.

No data is transmitted to third-party servers controlled by the developer.

The extension does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Permissions

The extension may require permissions necessary to:

  • Access Actionstep pages
  • Read and modify page content required for onboarding workflows
  • Upload and process CSV files locally
  • Copy lookup information to the clipboard

These permissions are used solely to provide the extension's functionality.

Third-Party Services

This extension interacts only with the Actionstep platform used by the user.

Please refer to Actionstep’s own privacy policies for information regarding their handling of data.

Security

The extension is designed to operate entirely within the user's browser session and does not intentionally collect or retain sensitive information externally.

Users are responsible for securing access to their own devices and Actionstep accounts.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the extension after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
